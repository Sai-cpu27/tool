How to Optimize Your Images

Our image compression tool helps you reduce file size while maintaining visual quality. Follow these steps:

  1. Upload your image (drag & drop or click to select)
  2. Adjust compression level (higher % = better quality)
  3. Choose output format (WebP recommended for smallest size)
  4. Click "Download" to save your optimized image

Why Compress Images?

  • Faster websites: Smaller images load quicker, improving user experience
  • Save bandwidth: Reduce data usage for mobile visitors
  • Better SEO: Page speed is a ranking factor for search engines
  • Storage savings: Store more images with less space
